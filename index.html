Looksmaxxing Challenges

Looksmaxxing Challenges force a rizzmax reset (YOU WILL NOT GAIN RIZZ POINTS FROM THIS!!). This means it is heavily recommended that you rizzmax before starting one of these.
In a looksmaxxing challenge, all your Rizzmax Upgrades are disabled, and you must follow restrictions whilst trying to reach a requirement.
After meeting this requirement, and ending the challenge, you will gain permanent bonuses usable outside of a Looksmaxxing Challenge.
As you complete the challenges more and more, the requirements for them go up more and more.
You are unable to Rizzmax or spend Rizz Points whilst in a Looksmaxxing Challenge.

You can only have one looksmaxxing challenge active at a time.

For every looksmaxxing challenge completion, you will gain a permanent +5% Dilyan Point Multiplier that works inside and outside of Looksmaxxing Challenges.
